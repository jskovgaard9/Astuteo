A website overhaul only goes so far. This year’s 40% boost in traffic begins to settle back down when you move on to the next project on your to-do list. Digital marketing is deceiving that way. When magazines get thrown in the trash, when billboards come down, when radio ads stop running, you notice the void. You don’t notice the lost potential of a website.
